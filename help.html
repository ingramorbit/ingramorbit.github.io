Help & Support

Answers to common questions and how to reach us.

How do I get leads?

Use the Get Leads Now button on the home page and complete the form. We’ll email details to leads@assistkingonline.com.

Can I contact you directly?

Yes — email leads@assistkingonline.com.

Having trouble with a page?

Try a hard refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R). If it persists, send a screenshot to leads@assistkingonline.com.
