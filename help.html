Common questions
How do I request a service?

Go to the service page you need, then choose the request button to submit your service request.

How do I check my request status?

Open My Requests to review the requests connected to your account.

How do contractors get leads?

Contractors need to complete onboarding, upload required documents, and receive approval before getting leads.

What should I send if a page is broken?

Send a screenshot, the page name, what button you clicked, and what happened.
